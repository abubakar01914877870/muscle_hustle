- Bolder weights throughout (600-800)

## Psychology Behind Changes

### Red/Orange (Primary Colors)
- Increases heart rate and excitement
- Proven to enhance workout performance
- Creates sense of urgency and action
- Motivates high-intensity behavior

### Dark Background
- Reduces eye strain during workouts
- Creates immersive, focused experience
- Makes bright colors pop more
- Modern, premium feel

### Electric Blue (Accent)
- Represents technology and data accuracy
- Provides contrast against warm colors
- Modern, futuristic vibe
- Trust in tracking/metrics

### Bold Typography
- Easier to read during movement
- Conveys strength and confidence
- Creates clear hierarchy
- Demands attention

## Accessibility Maintained

- High contrast ratios (WCAG AA compliant)
- Focus states with visible rings
- Keyboard navigation support
- Readable font sizes
- Color not sole indicator
